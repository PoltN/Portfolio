About

Hybrid Business × Tech × Product × UX profile — at the intersection of vision, strategy and execution.

My name is Emilio Cagnot, I'm 21. Born in Paris, I grew up in Barcelona — 7 years at the French Lycée in an international, bilingual environment — before returning to France to join Epitech Digital, then HETIC (Grande École Programme H3), and soon the MITIC Master's at Université Gustave Eiffel.

What defines me: I'm comfortable across strategy and technical execution, design and marketing alike. Neither purely creative nor purely analytical — I connect all dimensions of a project. A profile built in the field, not in the classroom.

At 19, I landed my first freelance mission solo by directly contacting the editorial director of 2B Immobilier. I expanded the scope without being asked — OVH domain migration, DNS/SSL, GDPR compliance, local SEO. The mission is still ongoing 18 months later.

I learn by doing. React, Next.js, FlutterFlow, WordPress, Illustrator, SEO — almost all of my technical skills were acquired on real projects. Critical and demanding, I defend my ideas when I believe in them, and I prefer building from scratch over improving what already exists.

Outside of work, I'm a reader, music listener and culture consumer — sectors that genuinely interest me: tech, culture, music, fashion, media.

What I'm looking for: to be at the intersection of product vision, strategy and execution. Leading creative, technically ambitious projects in digital agencies, tech startups or companies with a strong product or cultural dimension.
